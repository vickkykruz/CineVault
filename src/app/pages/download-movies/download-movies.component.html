About Video File

Video files are files that hold digital video data on a computer. They are designed to store videos in a way that takes up less space without compromissing too much on quality.
A video file typically consists of a container , like an MP4 file, which holds both the video data and audio data. The video data is encoded in a specific format, such as H.264, while the audio data is encoded in a different format, like Opus. The combination of the video and audio formats allows the video fie to play back with synchronized audio.
In addition to video and audio, the container can also include other helpful information, such as subtitles or metadata like the video's title. This information is stored alongside the video and audio data, making it easier for users to organize and find the videos they want to watch.
